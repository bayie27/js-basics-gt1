About Us

De La Salle Lipa (DLSL), established in 1962, is a private Catholic Lasallian institution located in Lipa City, Batangas. Guided by the mission of the De La Salle Brothers, DLSL is committed to providing quality human and Christian education to all, aiming to build a society founded on equity, justice, and sustainable development.

With a vibrant campus community of approximately 9,000 students and 700 faculty members, DLSL offers a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence and holistic development.

Learn more about our mission and vision on our Mission & Vision page.

Admissions

Embark on your Lasallian journey with our streamlined admissions process. Whether you're a prospective student for basic education or higher education, DLSL welcomes you to be part of our dynamic community.

Application Steps:

  1. Online Application: Complete the application form through our Online Admission Portal.
  2. Submit Requirements: Provide necessary documents such as PSA Birth Certificate, 2x2 photo, and academic records.
  3. Interview: Participate in an interview as part of the assessment process.
  4. Recommendation: Submit an interactive recommendation form from a school official.

For detailed information on program-specific requirements and scholarship opportunities, please visit our Admissions Page.

Academics

Colleges and Programs:
